About Me

Cynthia has been teaching at the Forever Learning Institute since 2013. She has degrees in both Mathematics and Computer Science. She was a GAANN Fellow (Graduate Assistance in Areas of National Need) at the University of Notre Dame, where she worked with the Kaneb Learning Center to learn how to teach well. In her spare time, Cynthia loves putting together jigsaw puzzles.
